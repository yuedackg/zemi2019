Bhatti Pasal is a stone's throw from Leidseplein, Rembrandtplein, Koningsplein and The Dam Square, serves gratifying authentic Nepalese food. Bhatti Pasal are small eatery in narrow alleyway of Nepal, which serves authentic and delicious Nepalese food.


momo


soup momo

Momo is a type of South Asian dumpling, popular across the Indian subcontinent and the Himalayan regions of broader South Asia. Momos are native to Tibet, Bhutan, Nepal, North Indian region of Ladakh, Northeast Indian regions of Sikkim, Assam, and Arunachal Pradesh,and Darjeeling. It is similar to Chinese baozi and jiaozi, Mongolian buuz, Japanese gyoza and Korean mandu, but heavily influenced by cuisine of the Indian subcontinent with Indian spices and herbs.
