About ANFA

the ALL NEPAL FOOTBALL ASSOCIATION (ANFA) is the governing body of football in nepal. it is responsible for the national team as well as club football. the organisations was founded in 1951 as the All Nepal Football Association and became affiliated with FIFA in 1972. The current President of ANFA is newly elected Karma Tsering Sherpa.Its current headquarters is located in the ANFA House in ANFA Complex, Satdobato.
