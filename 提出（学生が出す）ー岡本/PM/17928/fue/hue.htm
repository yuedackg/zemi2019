    • Trang chu

    Am thuc Viet Nam khong gianh diem vi su phuc tap. Nhieu trong so nhung mon an pho bien nhat co the duoc nau ngay ben ve duong cung ngon lanh nhu trong nha hang thuong hang. Nhung chinh su don gian nay, cach bien the mon an tinh te theo vung va nguuyen lieu tuoi xanh khien chung ta cu muon an them nua.

    Mon an

    1.Mon an mien bac

    Pho Ha Noi

    Pho Ha Noi la mon an dai dien cho am thuc Viet Nam da co mat tren toan the gioi va duoc danh gia la mot mon awn dac bit chi co o Viet Nam.

    Bun cay Hai Phong

    Bun ca cay Hai Phong la mon an mang huong vi dong que. Bun ca cay Hai Phong co huong vi nuoc dung va duoc an cung mieng ca ro gionf rum, cha ca thu dai dai mem ngot dam da vi ca va phai an cung rau doc mung gion gion.

    Vit quay Van Dinh

    Vit co Van Dinh khong chi ngon tu huong vi tam uop ma ngon tu chinh nguyen lieu "vit co".Vit co Van Dinh ban co the cam nhan duoc so thit mong,mem, ngot.

    2.Mon an mien Trung

    Bun bo Hue

    To bun bo Hue gay thuong nho bang thu nuoc dung dam da thom mui mam ruoc, thit bo mem,gio heo mieng lon day dan va cha cua thom ngot dac trung.

    Nem lui xu Hue

    Lui nuong thom phuc, khi an kem voi rau song,banh trang. Nem lui hap dan den noi ngay nay rat nhieu quan an via he den nha hang dac san 3 mien deu dua mon nay vao thuc don.

    Com ga

    Dac trung cua com ga la hat com toi,thom,ngay do duoc nau cung nuoc luoc ga.kem theo do laf ga ta chac thit xe, tron cung hanh tay,rau rawm thom nuc,dam vi.

    3.Mon an mien Nam

    Com tam

    Com tam thuong duoc an voi 4 mon chinh:suon lon nuong, bi lon, cha, trung op la. Tren nen com tam trnag boc khoi laf mau vang cuarmieng suon nuong, mau trang duc cuar soi bi dai mem, mieng cha duoc dat ben canh trung op la vua chin khien nguoi an nho mai.

    Cha gio

    Cha gio mien Nam bao gom nhieu loai: cha gio chay, cha gio trai cay, cha gio hai san, cha gio hoanh thanh, cha gio re, cha ram, tuy theo nguyen lieu ma chon phu gia thic hop.
